Narges Pourjafarian

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Human-Computer Interaction Lab at Saarland University, supervised by Prof. Jürgen Steimle. My research focus is on human-computer interaction (HCI) with interests in personal fabrication, embedded systems, and customized sensors. As part of my doctoral research, I have presented novel digital design and fabrication approaches for customized interactive interfaces.

Previously, I received two master's degrees, the first in electrical engineering at Shiraz University and the second in computer science at Saarland University. During my first master's degree, I worked on the evaluation of a class of optimization algorithms.
